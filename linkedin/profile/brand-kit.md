## Logo

   - Background: White only

- **Documents:** Top-left or center, depending on layout
- **Videos:** Bottom-right corner, 30% opacity during video, full opacity at end

‚ùå Don't stretch or distort the logo
‚ùå Don't change logo colors outside brand palette

## Typography

### Primary Font: **Inter**
- **Headings:** Inter Bold (700), Inter Extra Bold (800)
- **Body Text:** Inter Regular (400), Inter Medium (500)
- **Captions:** Inter Light (300)
- **Source:** Google Fonts
- **Fallback:** -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", sans-serif

### Secondary Font: **Source Sans Pro**
- **Usage:** Alternative for documents and presentations
- **Weights:** Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600), Bold (700)
- **Source:** Google Fonts

### Font Usage Guidelines

**Headings (H1-H3):**
- Font: Inter Bold or Extra Bold
- Size: 32px - 48px (Desktop), 24px - 36px (Mobile)
- Line height: 1.2
- Letter spacing: -0.02em

**Body Text:**
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 16px (Desktop), 14px (Mobile)
- Line height: 1.6
- Letter spacing: Normal

**Captions & Small Text:**
- Font: Inter Regular or Light
- Size: 12px - 14px
- Line height: 1.4
- Color: Medium Gray (#6B7280)

## Visual Style

### Design Principles
1. **Minimalist** ‚Äî Clean, uncluttered layouts
2. **Data-Driven** ‚Äî Use charts, graphs, and data visualizations
3. **Neural Network Theme** ‚Äî Subtle network patterns in backgrounds
4. **Professional** ‚Äî Corporate and trustworthy aesthetic
5. **Tech-Forward** ‚Äî Modern, cutting-edge visual language

### Graphic Elements

**Neural Network Pattern**
- Style: Connected dots and lines
- Colors: LyDian Blue with opacity (10-20%)
- Usage: Background overlays, headers, cards

**Data Flow Lines**
- Style: Flowing curved lines with gradient
- Colors: Blue gradient (LyDian Blue ‚Üí Light Blue)
- Usage: Connecting elements, showing data flow

**Geometric Shapes**
- Style: Rounded rectangles, circles
- Colors: Brand colors with gradient or solid fill
- Usage: Icons, buttons, highlights

**Gradients**
- Primary: LyDian Blue (#00B4FF) ‚Üí Dark Gray (#121826)
- Secondary: Purple (#8B5CF6) ‚Üí Pink (#EC4899)
- Usage: Backgrounds, headers, CTAs

## Brand Voice & Messaging

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional** ‚Äî Corporate and credible
- **Approachable** ‚Äî Friendly but not casual
- **Innovative** ‚Äî Forward-thinking and tech-savvy
- **Trustworthy** ‚Äî Reliable and transparent
- **Educational** ‚Äî Informative without being condescending

### Key Messages
1. "Intelligence for Every Domain"
2. "Multi-model AI for maximum flexibility"
3. "Built on Azure, trusted by enterprises"
4. "20+ languages, global reach"
5. "Privacy-first, white-hat security"

### Writing Guidelines
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences short and clear
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include CTAs in all marketing content
- Be transparent about AI limitations
